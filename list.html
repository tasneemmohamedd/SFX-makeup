Materials you may need

  • Scar wax
  • Fake blood
  • Liquid latex
  • White glue
  • Cotton
  • Vasline
  • Eyeshadow or Lipsticks

Scar wax:

It's the fake skin you use instead of your hands, it's like a dough which you can make at home , it's made of white flour , vasline, and foundation that matches your skin color . Mix them all together until you get the desired result

Fake blood:

It's the fake blood you use, it can be made of honey and a little bit of red food coloring

Liquid latex:

It's literally like your skin , but it's a transparent liquid , that takes the skin color and it can be used with cotton if you want to cut it open. It is typically applied using a disposable sponge and takes about five to ten minutes to dry depending on how thick it is applied. As it dries, it solidifies to a rubbery consistency and in the process ends up shrinking by approximately 3%.

Glue:

White glue or UHU glue can give you the result of a burning effect , you keep moving your hands up and down side to side until it forms a wrenckled shape. To give you the burning color effect try using different shades of red eyeshadow, or lipstick and blend it well.

The difference between liquid latex and scar wax:

They both can give the desired product , but they share alot of differences.

Liquid latex is a liquid that makes fake skin on your skin and to open it up you need to use cotton with it . It also can be used on your eyes , and those sensitive parts of the body.

Scar wax is a litle bit different , its more like a soft smooth dough that you can spread on your skin using vasline and it can be homemade while liquid latex can't . Scar wax can be used on the hands as well as the liquid latex , but it can be hard to use it on your eyes. For me i prefer using scar wax .
